How EduSearch AI Works

Our intelligent retrieval system follows a streamlined process to deliver accurate information

1

Document Processing

Regulations, policies, and documents are broken into smaller chunks and converted into numerical representations (embeddings).

2

Vector Storage

These embeddings are stored in a specialized vector database optimized for similarity search.

3

Query Processing

When a question is asked, it's converted to embeddings and matched against the database.

4

Answer Generation

Retrieved document chunks are sent to an LLM to generate precise, human-readable answers.

Query: "What are the new guidelines for research project funding in 2023?"

Answer: According to the 2023 Research Funding Guidelines issued by the Department of Higher Education, research project funding has been updated with the following key changes:

  • Increased funding allocation for interdisciplinary research projects by 15%
  • Simplified application process with a new single-window clearance system
  • Extended eligibility to include private institutions with NAAC A+ grade or higher
  • New focus areas include climate change studies, AI in education, and sustainable development
  • Mandatory inclusion of early-career researchers in project teams

The complete guidelines document (REF/GDL/2023/07) was published on March 15, 2023, and applies to all funding applications submitted after April 1, 2023.
